Who We Are

About the Department

The Department of Geophysics at Mountain Top University is dedicated to the scientific study of the Earth through physical principles. We train the next generation of geophysicists equipped with strong theoretical knowledge and practical field skills.

Our curriculum blends seismology, exploration geophysics, environmental geophysics, and applied remote sensing, preparing graduates for careers in the oil & gas industry, environmental consulting, academia, and government agencies.

Rooted in the university's motto — Empowered to Excel — our department fosters innovation, integrity, and academic excellence in every student.

Our Leadership

HOD & Executives

Dr. Okunubi Michael Olajide

Head of Department

Dr. Okunubi Michael Olajide research focuses on mapping subsurface features favorable to the accumulation of mineral resources, with a parallel interest in computational geophysics. His methodological expertise spans potential field methods gravity and magnetic surveying as well as a broad suite of electrical techniques, including Self-Potential (SP), Induced Polarisation (IP), Resistivity (RES), and select Electromagnetic (EM) methods.

Through the integration of these complementary approaches, his work delivers robust characterisation of subsurface geology, supporting resource exploration and advancing computational frameworks in applied geophysics.

Research Focus Areas

🌍

Exploration Geophysics

Subsurface mapping for hydrocarbon and mineral resources using seismic, gravity, and magnetic methods.

💧

Environmental Geophysics

Investigation of groundwater systems, soil contamination, and near-surface hazards using geophysical techniques.

📡

Remote Sensing & GIS

Application of satellite data and geographic information systems to geological and geophysical mapping.

🏗️

Engineering Geophysics

Site investigation and structural integrity assessment in support of civil engineering projects.
