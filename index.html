About the Department

The Department of Geosciences, Mountain Top University offers a strong and focused training in Geology and Geophysics, giving students a solid understanding of the Earth from both a physical and interpretational perspective. In Geology, students explore how rocks form, how sedimentary basins evolve, and how structures like faults and folds control the distribution of natural resources. In Geophysics, the focus shifts to using physical methods to investigate what lies beneath the surface without digging through techniques such as seismic, gravity, magnetic, and electrical surveys.

Together, these two areas equip students with the knowledge needed to understand subsurface conditions, evaluate natural resources, and solve real-world geological problems, especially in energy and environmental sectors. What makes the department stand out is its strong emphasis on practical, hands-on learning and industry-relevant skills. Students are trained to use modern interpretation and modeling software such as Petrel, ArcGIS, Hampson-Russell, and Shearwater, allowing them to analyze seismic data, build subsurface models, and make informed geological predictions.

In addition, the department is equipped with a range of field and laboratory instruments for data acquisition, including gravimeters, magnetometers, SuperSting R8, SuperSting R1, Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR), and more. These tools give students real experience in collecting and interpreting geophysical data, bridging the gap between classroom theory and field application. Overall, the department provides a well-rounded, practical, and forward-thinking education designed to prepare students for careers in geoscience, energy, environmental management, and beyond.

Professional Affiliations

Student Chapters

Our department hosts active student chapters of leading professional organizations, including NAPE, NMGS, SEG, SPE, and AAPG. These chapters provide students with opportunities for professional development, technical training, networking, and industry exposure through workshops, seminars, field activities, and conferences serving as a bridge between academic learning and real-world geoscience practice.

Nigeria

NAPE

Nigerian Association of Petroleum Explorationists

Focuses on petroleum exploration and geoscience practice in Nigeria. Brings together geologists, geophysicists, and engineers in the oil and gas sector.

Nigeria

NMGS

Nigerian Mining and Geosciences Society

Covers a broader scope of geosciences including mining, geology, and earth sciences in Nigeria.

International

SEG

Society of Exploration Geophysicists

An international organization focused on applied geophysics, especially in energy exploration.

International

SPE

Society of Petroleum Engineers

A global professional body for engineers involved in the exploration and production of oil and gas.

International

AAPG

American Association of Petroleum Geologists

An international association dedicated to petroleum geology, research, and professional development.

Research Focus Areas

🌍

Exploration Geophysics

Subsurface mapping for hydrocarbon and mineral resources using seismic, gravity, and magnetic methods.

💧

Environmental Geophysics

Investigation of groundwater systems, soil contamination, and near-surface hazards using geophysical techniques.

📡

Remote Sensing & GIS

Application of satellite data and geographic information systems to geological and geophysical mapping.

🏗️

Engineering Geophysics

Site investigation and structural integrity assessment in support of civil engineering projects.
